My Personal Interests

I love music and creating music. I started producing my own music when I was 17 (I am 24 now) and I absolutely love it. I mostly make Hip-Hop tempo beats, often with an electronic touch, but really I'll make whatever I'm feeling. I haven't released any of my music anywhere, but someday, if I feel like it's good enough, I might.

Another interest of mine is technology. Generally speaking, I love all forms of technology: computers, software, video games, etc. I grew up fairly poor, so as a 10 year old determined to have a computer of my own, I managed to build my own computer out of scrap computer parts that I thrifted from 3 different broken machines. The rest is history!

Recently, I discovered that I love working on my car. Car stuff always seemed intimidating to me- mostly because I've never felt like I fit in with your typical mechanic type of person. In 2022 my car broke down and I had to use my small savings to purchase an old 2006 Subaru outback with 200,000 miles on it. Needless to say, owning a car with that many miles has forced me to learn how to maintain many different components of an engine.
